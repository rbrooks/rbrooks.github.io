Sketches

April 15th, 2007

Clearly there were only two things on my mind in my late teens / early twenties.

Cliff Girl, Pianitar, and Geddy Lee were created when I was a Junior in high school, 1986. Rush was created when I was 19. (So I was a little obsessed with that band.) Some Girl and Julia were created 6 years later in the mid-90’s. The medium used on all but Pianitar was good ol’, school-grade #2 pencil. Pianitar was pen and ink.

These are just about the only sketches I’ve done in my life. I suppose I should “analog” draw more than I do.
